Educational & Reference Use Only

This application is a reference and educational tool only. It is not intended to serve as a source of medical advice, treatment orders, or authorization to perform any clinical intervention.

No Treatment Authorization — Access to this application does not grant scope-of-practice privileges, medical director approval, or permission to perform any patient care procedure. All clinical decisions must be made in accordance with your agency's currently issued protocols, your certifications, and direct medical oversight.

Accuracy Not Guaranteed — Protocol information, medication dosages, and clinical guidelines contained in this app may not reflect your jurisdiction's most current authorized protocols. Always defer to your agency's official documents and medical director guidance.

Clinical Judgment Required — This application does not replace formal EMS education, licensure, certification, continuing education, or clinical judgment. Use it as a supplement to — never a substitute for — proper training and oversight.

Proprietary Content — Content in this application may be proprietary to your organization. Unauthorized reproduction or distribution outside of authorized channels is strictly prohibited.
